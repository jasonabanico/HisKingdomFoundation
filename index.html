Who We Are

At His Kingdom Foundation (HKF), we envision a society where every individual has the opportunity to thrive and experience compassion and support. We strive to empower the marginalised, protect the vulnerable, and uplift the disadvantaged in our community. Through our work, we bring hope, healing, and restoration to those in need, addressing practical needs with hands-on assistance and essential services. We aim to be a beacon of light and transformation in our area to build a culture of compassion, justice, and love. Join us in our mission to create a brighter future and make a difference in our community. His Kingdom Foundation - bringing hope, healing, and transformation to those we serve.

Elderly and Vulnerable Support

We are committed to raising awareness of issues affecting the elderly, such as dementia, through engaging events at aged care facilities. These events provide a platform for professionals to share their expertise, offer practical guidance, and foster meaningful conversations with residents and their families. Our initiatives are designed to promote understanding, create a sense of community, and support the emotional and mental well-being of the elderly.

Family Violence

Our primary focus is supporting women and families affected by family violence. Our long-term vision is to become a one-stop resource, offering safe accommodation to those fleeing violence who cannot access emergency housing. We aim to bridge this critical gap, ensuring women and children can escape unsafe situations without delay. Once they are secure, we provide practical support to help them rebuild, including assistance in finding long-term housing and establishing a stable future, recognising that many leave with little more than courage and hope.
